Service Areas — Region 02

East Tennessee & the Tri-Cities

Our original Tennessee territory, and still where our drone LiDAR platform earns its keep the most — steep grades and heavy canopy that make ground crews slow and safety a real consideration.

Coverage

Knox, Sullivan, Washington, Carter, Unicoi, Greene & Hawkins Counties

This is where Ascension's Tennessee work started, and it remains the region we know best on the ground — from the Tri-Cities (Johnson City, Bristol, Kingsport) out through the more rural mountain counties toward Knoxville.

The terrain here — steep grades, dense forest cover, limited road access on larger rural parcels — is exactly the case where a single drone LiDAR flight can capture in hours what a ground crew would spend days traversing safely. We lean on that platform heavily for topographic and terrain work in this region.

Tennessee boundary and platting standards are set by the Tennessee State Board of Examiners for Land Surveyors; recorded plats are filed at the county Register of Deeds.
