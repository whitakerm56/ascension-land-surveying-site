NC Piedmont Triad

Home base. Mocksville, Winston-Salem, Greensboro, High Point — and the region where Ascension has the deepest local relationships and fastest response time.

Coverage

Forsyth, Davie, Yadkin, Guilford, Rowan, Iredell, Surry, Catawba & Caswell Counties

Based in Mocksville (Davie County), this is the region we've served longest and know best — recording offices, permitting quirks, and the local engineering firms we work alongside regularly.

Boundary surveys and plats here follow the standards set by the NC Board of Examiners for Engineers and Surveyors, and recorded subdivision plats are filed with the county Register of Deeds. County-specific setback and easement requirements shape how a boundary survey gets used in permitting, which is worth flagging early on land development projects.
