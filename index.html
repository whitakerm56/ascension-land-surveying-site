How a Project Runs

From courthouse research to recorded plat.

N 45°28′16″ E — Mocksville, NC

  1. Records Research

    We pull deed history, prior plats, and county GIS data before anyone sets foot on the property. Field time spent verifying, not searching.

    ▶ Courthouse · County GIS · NCDOT right-of-way records

  2. Field Data Collection

    RTK GNSS, total station, and drone LiDAR flights capture boundary evidence and terrain data matched to the accuracy the project actually requires.

    ▶ RTK GNSS · Total Station · DJI Matrice LiDAR

  3. Office Processing

    Point clouds classified, boundary evidence analyzed against record documents, survey drafted to state minimum standards under the PLS seal.

    ▶ LiDAR classification · CAD drafting · PLS review & seal

  4. Delivery & Recording

    Stamped drawings and data in the formats your team needs — PDF, DWG, or point cloud — with plats recorded at the county Register of Deeds where required.

    ▶ PDF · DWG · LAS point cloud · County recording
