Wake, Durham, Orange & Chatham Counties

The Triangle's growth, driven heavily by Research Triangle Park and the tech and biotech employers around it, means a steady pipeline of land development and civil engineering survey work — ALTA/NSPS surveys for commercial acquisitions, topographic surveys ahead of site design, and construction staking on active build-out.

Boundary surveys and plats here follow the standards set by the NC Board of Examiners for Engineers and Surveyors, and recorded subdivision plats are filed with the county Register of Deeds.
